About Me

Hello! I'm Divya Jyoti Bajpai, a Ph.D. (PMRF) Research Scholar at the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, IIT Bombay. My research focuses on Inference Latency Reduction in Generative Models, specifically targeting Large Language Models (LLMs) and Diffusion Models.

I develop novel techniques leveraging Early Exits, Speculative Decoding, and Flow Matching to achieve faster and more reliable AI inference. My work explores distributed inference strategies across mobile devices, edge servers, and cloud infrastructure, making large-scale AI models more accessible and efficient.

I have collaborated with leading industry researchers at Adobe Research India and Amazon‚ÄìIIT Bombay AI/ML Initiative, publishing extensively at top-tier conferences including ICLR, NeurIPS, ACL, EMNLP, and others. My vision is to create AI systems that are efficient, trustworthy, and accessible to everyone.

Beyond research, I enjoy playing cricket, exploring online games, and listening to music.
